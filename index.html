Why have French Bulldogs become so popular?

Frenchies are affectionate, friendly dogs that were bred to be companions. Although they're somewhat slow to be housebroken, they get along well with other dogs and aren't big barkers. The dogs don't need much exercise, so they are fine in small areas and enjoy the safety of a crate.

Should I buy a French Bulldog?

YES!!!!. French Bulldogs are life changing Dogs. From their small compact size , to their temperment , to their cuteness.

How much does a french bulldog cost?

Priceless! Price is something to consider , however , you pay for what you get. Prices vary depending on bloodline. 2021 Prices range from €1000-€3000. Of course there are some dogs which can fetch as far as €20000!!!

Are french bulldogs lazy?

As Lazy as their owner and as Active as their owner. French Bulldogs love long walks , short walks , beach walks , you name it! They are also avid lovers of lazy days. So whatever your level of activity , your Frenchie will adpat. Just to note , frenchies do not deal well in hot weather , so best to avoid walsk during the day in hot countries.

Are french bulldogs easy to train?

French Bulldogs can be easy to train, but they can also be stubborn. Be firm and patient when training this breed. ... Although it is important to always supervise young children and dogs when they are together, the French Bulldog does very well with children.
